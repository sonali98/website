Nothing in this world is easy to develop right from the scratch, and that too in the whole new environment. It's not easy to implement someone's idea into another mind. But this is where the true connection of artist takes place. Like every other giant company,this also started in a classroom with few founder members. And it started right after the holidays. The Spectrum Boom was everywhere in the college. It was just like the new season playing in the campus. Artists was working with amazing enthusiasm. And after all, it resulted with a great bang. almost hundreds of spectrum members on the stage inaugurating something new and "never happened before in the campus". And after that we never looked back.Back to back performances from all the sections leaves impressions on everybody's mind in such a manner that Art Spectrum became official cultural group of PVPIT, Budhgaon.

   After that, the most innovating and biggest cultural event "Vasant Karandak" helped Art Spectrum to grow outside the campus. The FlashMob, Street plays on Ganesh Utsav, the very first Kala Mahotsav, Sangli International Film Festival and many more events were successfully organized and executed by Art Spectrum. Now, we are working in the 6th season of Art Spectrum. We have reached from a small town of Sangli to the Kolhapur, Satara, Pune, Mumbai and nearby areas. And this happened only because of you.Keep supporting us, loving us and see you at our biggest cultural event "Vasant Karandak 2k19".
